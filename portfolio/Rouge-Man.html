ABOUT

Recreating Pac-Man

Creating this project included recreating Pac-Man. A highlight of this process was figuring out the simple AI of the ghosts.


Making Pac-Man a Rogue-like

To make Pac-Man a rouge-like we added the option of collecting multiple items to create variations between games. These items had varying effects: to adding lives, speeding up the character, increasing the size of their collection radius, etc.


Maintaining Version Notes

This game was made as part of an IMGD course with an emphasis on learning about industry and producion skills. As such throughout the creation of this game, maintaining accurate and standardized version notes was a must.
